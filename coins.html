What is coins?

coins is a new gamification system to reward you for your participation and achievements within HackerTribe.

By competing in various hackathons and performing certain tasks at HackerTribe, you can earn coins, which can then be swapped for awesome prizes and exclusive offers.

This way, your contributions to HackerTribe are recognized with tangible rewards and benefits.

What Quests can I complete to earn coins?

Here are a few of the Quests that will grant you coins:

  • Updating your profile: Tweak your profile and make sure it’s up to date.
  • Completing onboarding tasks: Get started with easy tasks to earn your first coins.
  • Winning hackathons Showcase your skills and win big by participating in hackathons .
  • Building a team : Team creation for a hackathon by the team leader at HackTribe rewards you with the coins.

There are plenty of Quests waiting to be completed and you’ll find them here.

FAQs

To help you navigate the coins system, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions.

This section will provide you with all the essential information you need to make the most out of earning and using coins within HackerTribe.

What do I need to earn coins?

A: Just log in to HackerTribe,update your profile, and start participation in hackathons.

What can I do with coins?

A: You'll be able to trade coins for hackathon participation and exciting prizes. We will announce the rewards soon.

What rewards can I get with my coins?

A: The rewards will include token rewards and other exciting incentives, which we are currently developing and will announce in due time.

Are coins a cryptocurrency?

A: No, coins are not a cryptocurrency. They are a gamification system currency, which will enable you to redeem them for various rewards.

Is there a limit to how many coins I can earn?

A: There is no specified limit mentioned, so keep competing in hackathons to rack up as many coins as possible!

Can I transfer my coins to another user?

A: This feature is not available. Please stay tuned for updates on any new features.

Do I need to pay to participate in earning coins?

A: No, participating in earning coins through Quests is free. Just complete the listed Quests to start earning.

Can coins be used outside of HackerTribe?

A: coins are currently intended for use within the HackerTribe ecosystem to participate in hackathons.
